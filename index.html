    What is an Introducing Broker (IB)?

    An introducing broker (IB) is a trusted partner that connects clients to TMGM and earns commissions based on their trading activity. IBs leverage their network and expertise to earn volume-based commissions on every trade their referred clients make through the IB program.

    Ads don't build trust anymore. Trading is personal money, and people take it to someone they trust — not a banner. As a TMGM IB you don't need an ad budget or a licence to start; you need the network you already have. And because every client you refer carries your name, the broker either honours it or burns it. That's what the five questions below are for.

    Introducing Broker FAQ

    An introducing broker (IB) is a trusted partner that connects clients to TMGM and earns commissions based on their trading activity. IBs leverage their network and expertise to earn volume-based commissions on every trade their referred clients make through the IB program.

    Becoming an introducing broker is simple: 1) Apply via the TMGM Partners website and join the IB program; 2) Receive your personalized IB broker dashboard and referral link; 3) Promote TMGM using your network and our marketing tools; 4) Earn daily commissions from your clients' trading activity. Everything is tracked in real time.

    Joining the IB program comes with powerful benefits: High Earnings — tiered rebates, customised to your book; Fast Payouts — daily withdrawals with no minimums; Sub-IB Network — build your team and increase your revenue; Dedicated Support — a personal IB broker account manager; VIP Access — exclusive events like Chelsea FC match experiences; Marketing Tools — custom assets for IB growth.

    To join the IB program, you should have basic trading knowledge and a network of potential clients. It's ideal for financial professionals, educators and market influencers. You're expected to support your clients, follow industry standards and comply with regulations. No licence is required to get started, but professionalism is a must.

    Your rewards are based on the total trading volume (measured in lots) of your referred clients. The IB program offers tiered commission rates, with real-time tracking through your IB broker dashboard. Commissions are paid daily.
